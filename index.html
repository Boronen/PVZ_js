How to Play

Defend your lawn from waves of zombies using plants!

  • ☀️ Sun — Collect sun to plant defenders. Sunflowers produce extra sun.
  • 🌱 Draft — Each run starts with a random selection of 5 plants. Pick 3.
  • 🧟 Waves — Survive 10 waves of increasingly dangerous zombies.
  • 🛒 Shop — After each wave, spend coins on powerful roguelike perks.
  • ⭐ Elites — Every 3rd wave spawns a mutated elite zombie.
  • 💀 Permadeath — If zombies reach your house, the run ends!
  • 🌰 Seeds — Earn seeds each run to permanently unlock new plants.
